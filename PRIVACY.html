Privacy Policy for EasyCut

Last Updated: February 15, 2026

Overview

EasyCut is a desktop application for downloading YouTube videos and recording live streams. This policy explains how data is handled.

Information We Collect

  • OAuth access tokens (stored locally in config/youtube_token.pickle)
  • YouTube cookies (stored locally in config/yt_cookies.txt)
  • Download history (stored locally in config/history_downloads.json)
  • Application settings (stored locally in config/config.json)
  • Application logs (stored locally in config/app.log)

All data is stored locally on your computer. No data is sent to external servers.

Data Usage

  • Authenticate with YouTube via OAuth 2.0
  • Download videos you request
  • Record live streams you specify

Legal Compliance and User Responsibility

EasyCut is intended for personal, non-commercial use only.

  • Download your own videos or content with explicit permission.
  • Follow YouTube Terms of Service and copyright laws.

Users are responsible for compliance and legal use of downloaded content.
